# College Counselor - Product Enhancement Ideas

## Vision
Transform the College Counselor into a comprehensive, multi-year college admissions planning platform that guides students from 8th/9th grade through college acceptance, helping them strategically build their profile, discover best-fit colleges, and maximize their admissions success.
